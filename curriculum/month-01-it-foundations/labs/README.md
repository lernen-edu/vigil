# Month 1 Labs

Three labs, done in order. Each has its own folder with a full spec. The lab attempt floor and the no-AI rule apply to every one.

| Lab | Folder | Time budget | Floor | What you build |
| --- | ------ | ----------- | ----- | -------------- |
| 1.1 Hardware Inventory | `lab-01-hardware-inventory/` | 8 to 10 h | 60 min | A Bash inventory script and the habit of enumerating any host |
| 1.2 EFI Shell and the Boot Process | `lab-02-efi-shell-boot/` | 10 to 12 h | 60 min | A corrected mental model of the boot chain, firmware to kernel |
| 1.3 picoCTF General Skills | `lab-03-picoctf-general-skills/` | 10 to 12 h | 45 min per stuck challenge | Bare-hands shell fluency and the no-flag-confirmation habit |

Do all three, commit a notebook entry for each, and write the end-of-month boot-process deliverable (see `../deliverable.md`) before you advance to Month 2.
